# User Stories

If this first user used EndoMatrix for 30 days, what would make them say:
**“This finally helped me understand something I couldn’t see before.”**

## User Story 1: Recognizing Patterns
**“I’m not imagining this. The same symptoms show up in the same phases over and over,
and they escalate in predictable ways. That means I can plan around them instead of being blindsided.”**

## User Story 2: Anticipating Difficult Days
**Seeing consistent, repeatable symptom patterns across cycle stages that explain why my pain, 
fatigue, and mood shift when they do, so I can anticipate difficult days and adjust my routines 
instead of being caught off guard.**

## User Story 3: Planning and Self-Care
**Realizing that my symptoms aren’t random or purely “bad periods,” but follow repeatable patterns 
across my cycle that I can anticipate reopening every month, helping me plan work, rest, and care 
before symptoms escalate.**

## User Story 4: Reducing Severity and Articulating my experice to Clinicians
**Understanding that my symptoms follow long-term patterns tied to specific phases, not personal 
failure or randomness, which lets me anticipate flare-ups, reduce their severity, and communicate 
my experience more clearly to clinicians.**


## The litmus test
Would a person with endometriosis screenshot this sentence and say **“this is exactly it”**?
